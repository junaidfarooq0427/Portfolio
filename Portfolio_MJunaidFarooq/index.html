Linear Regression Analysis for Important Companies

The project involves a comprehensive regression analysis of the relationship between the performance of Important companies and the FTSE 100 index. The analysis examines the extent to which the FTSE 100 index can explain the variability in the stock prices of the selected companies, providing insights into the predictive capability of the Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM). The study covers a wide range of industries and sectors, offering a thorough examination of the applicability of CAPM across diverse market categories.
